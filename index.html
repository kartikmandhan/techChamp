About Us

Winners

In the biggest battle of the finest minds, it’s time to see who perseveres till the end. Strive for the ultimate glory! And this time it won’t be an easy climb!

Teams

Teams will participate in a series of competitions that will test their nerves in all disciplines of engineering.

Prizes

Winners will be bestowed with the grand title MindSpark'19 Champions along with the glorious Trophy & a whooping prize money in store.


Team Structure:

The Event will work on Four Groups of engineering stream, viz :

  1. Civil + Planning
  2. Mechanical + Production
  3. Computer + Information Technology
  4. Electrical + Electronics and Telecommunication + Instrumentation.

The Participating Colleges can send their teams in following ways:

  1. Team of Five (2 + 2 + 1) :

    Two students each from any two out of the four groups mentioned above. The Fifth member will strengthen any one of the two groups you send.

  2. Team of Seven (2 + 2 + 2 + 1) :

    Two students each from any three out of the four groups mentioned above. The Seventh member will strengthen any one of the three groups you send.

  3. Team of Nine (2 + 2 + 2 + 2 + 1) :

    Two students each from all the four groups mentioned above. The Ninth Member will strengthen any one of the four groups you send.

Qualification Rounds

1

Round 1 is an Online Round which will be conducted in the month of September, 2019. Four quizzes will be uploaded on the MindSpark website www.mind-spark.org, one for each of the four mentioned groups.

The students of each group are supposed to solve the given quiz in the time allotted. The syllabus for the quiz will be uploaded on the same website in advance. The link to the quiz will remain active for 20 days. However, each team will be allowed only one attempt per quiz.

2

Round 2 will be conducted at COEP during the Days of MindSpark’19. Four different events (one for each group) will be conducted. Details of the event will be disclosed on the day of the event.

However, any prerequisites will be mentioned along with Round 1 results on the website. Please note, team with 2 or 3 groups can participate in events of other branches.

3
Its the Championship Round!
Round 3 will be conducted at COEP during the days of MindSpark’19. Further details of this round will be disclosed to the qualified teams on the day of the event.

Evaluation Scheme:

  1. 1. Marks will be awarded out of 100.The highest scorer in each group will be awarded a 10 pointer.
  2. 2. Remaining participating teams in that particular group will be awarded a pointer with respect to the Score of the topper.

    Example: If the topper team for the civil group scores 68/100, the pointer for this team X will be 10. Another team, say Y with a score of 56, will be awarded a pointer with respect to the 68 of team X, i.e. (56/68)*10 = 8.2.

  3. 3. A boundary pointer (e.g. 4.5) will be chosen. All scores above this boundary pointer will be considered Pass ,the ones below will be considered Fail.
  1. 4. The Marking Scheme for round 3 will be disclosed on the day of the event.
  2. 5. Note: Teams with less than 4 groups are allowed and encouraged to appear for the exams / events of other groups in order to increase their chances of qualification. The organizing team will first check for such team's qualification according to criteria 5b or 5c in event book. If they fail to qualify in these, they will then be checked for 5a.
  3. 6. 2 out of 3 groups qualify in a Team of 7,
    3 out of 4 groups qualify in a Team of 9,
    2 out of 2 groups qualify in a Team of 5.

Team Structure

Colleges which have Engineering branches of all four groups mentioned above Will have to send a Team of 9.

Verification

All team members should come with valid college ID cards.

Rights

The organisers have the authority to change the above mentioned rules without any prior notice.

Participants

All Team Members should belong to First, Second or Third year of Engineering.

Discipline

All teams must maintain decorum at all times. Event organizers have a right to disqualify any team which behaves inappropriately.

Updates

Participants are requested to check the MindSpark’19 website (www.mind-spark.org) regularly for updates.

How can We Help You?

Q. Can I participate in other events at MindSpark'19 ?

A. You are allowed to participate in as many events at MindSpark'19 as long as you are able to manage your own schedule.

Q. Are there any prerequisites for Round 2 and 3 ?

A. Prerequisites and further details regarding Round 2 and 3 will be informed after the result of Round 1.

Q. What is the evaluation criterion for Round-1 ?

A. Team needs to get a minimum score(which will be conveyed before the online round) in each Branch test. The 10 teams with highest total score will be qualified for the next round.

Q. What about accommodation of Team from outside Pune ?

A. Accommodation will be provided free of cost in COEP Hostel to all the team members. Details of which will be disclosed later.

Q. Is a team of students from different colleges allowed ?

A. No. Team members should be from same college.
